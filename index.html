Climate change & Hurricanes

By far, major hurricanes are the world's most costly natural weather disaster. In some cases, it has caused over $100 billion in damage. Now, there is evidence that the human-caused global warming are leading to stronger and more destructive hurricanes. If the climate continues to warm, the latest research shows that the trend of more deadly hurricanes is likely to continue.
